Marinated Steak

Description

Enjoying a marinated steak on the weekend is one of the best meal in the world

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup red wine
  • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh thyme, or 2 teaspoons dried thyme
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper
  • 2 pounds top round boneless steak, cut 1-inch thick

Steps to make it

  1. In large shallow glass dish, combine wine, thyme, oil, garlic, salt, and pepper.
  2. Add steak and turn to coat.
  3. Cover with plastic wrap and marinate in refrigerator for 8 to 24 hours, turning steak occasionally to coat with marinade.
  4. Prepare and heat grill.
  5. Place steak on greased grill over hot coals.
  6. Brush with marinade to coat.
  7. Cover grill and cook steak for 7 to 8 minutes per side or until medium, or 145 F on a meat thermometer.
  8. Remove steaks from grill, cover, and let stand for 5 minutes before carving.
  9. Discard remaining marinade.
  10. Cut steak in half and refrigerate one of the halves until chilled; wrap and refrigerate for up to two days.
  11. Cut remaining steak into serving size pieces. You can also thinly slice remaining half across the grain on an angle and serve.
